#Divider (frame) Construction Instructions for CWZC Jan_2018

This folder contains instructions for the construction of dividers 2 & 3 based upon the construction of the first divider made (Dec 2017).

The lengths are in mm.

The timber used was rough construction timber which had a cross section of 38x19.

The 45 degree angles were cut using a mitre saw and cut with respect to the plane of the 38 wide face.

The total length of timber needed is 15800.
